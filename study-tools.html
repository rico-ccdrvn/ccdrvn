A curated set of tools and official resources for every stage of your Ontario licensing journey — from G1 knowledge test prep through to your G2 road test.

Stage 1 — G1 Knowledge Test

G1 Diagnostic Test

Not sure where to start? This short diagnostic identifies your weak spots in minutes, then personalizes what you study next.

↗ Free

G1 Practice Tests 1–3

Ontario-specific questions covering road rules, intersections, speed limits, distracted driving, and BAC rules. Hints and plain-English explanations included.

↗ Free

Road Signs Test

Every must-know sign you'll see on the G1 exam, with simplified explanations to help each one stick.

↗ Premium

G1 Exam Simulator

Mirrors the real test exactly — same question count, same passing score, no hints. New questions every attempt. Run it at least 3 times before your test date.

↗ Official — MTO

Ontario Driver's Handbook

The source of truth. All G1 questions are drawn directly from this handbook. Chapters 2, 3, and 6 are the most important for your written and road tests.

Stage 2 — G2 Road Test Prep

Richard's note: The hazard simulators below are some of the best free tools available for building the kind of scanning habit and situational awareness examiners look for. Use them in the weeks leading up to your road test.

Free

Hazard Simulators 1–7

Watch real traffic video clips and tap on developing hazards as they appear. Points are awarded for spotting them early — exactly like real defensive driving.

↗ Premium

Behind-the-Wheel Simulator

Real-world driving situations shown from the driver's seat perspective. Covers the everyday hazards and decisions you'll face during your road test.

↗ Premium

Virtual 360° Road Situations

Explore intersections, merges, and road scenarios from multiple angles. Helps you understand what to look for before you encounter it in real traffic.

↗ From Richard

Reference Material

Supplementary notes and guides I share with my students. Covers common road test errors, maneuver tips, and what examiners are actually watching for.
