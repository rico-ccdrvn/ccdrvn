My Lesson Review — AI Powered

Your Lesson,
Reviewed.

Every lesson with CC Driving Instruction can now include an AI-powered review of your drive. After your lesson, you receive a private link with short video clips of the moments that mattered, a scored report showing exactly where you stand, and a personalized practice plan matched to the skills you need to work on. It's like having your instructor ride along twice — once in the car, and once through the replay.

How It Works

Drive. Review. Improve.

STEP 1

You drive the lesson

Nothing changes in the car. You focus on driving while the in-vehicle system quietly records road view, speed, braking, and steering.

STEP 2

Your drive is analyzed

After the lesson, AI reviews the full drive against Ontario road test standards — observation checks, stops, turns, speed control, and more. Your instructor checks every report before it goes out.

STEP 3

You get your review

A private link arrives by email: your score, video clips of key moments, and step-by-step practice drills matched to exactly what you should practice next.

For Parents

Teaching Your Teen? The Practice Planner Is Your Co‑Pilot.

Most of a new driver's hours happen with a parent in the passenger seat — usually without a plan. Every lesson review generates a personal Practice Planner: exactly what to work on before the next lesson, built from what the AI and your instructor actually saw.

🗺️

A plan for every practice drive

Instead of "let's just drive around," you get specific drills drawn from the last lesson — the shoulder check that got missed, the stop that rolled.

🧑‍🏫

Coach from the plan

Each drill includes step-by-step "Do it right" coaching points, so you're coaching with the same standard the examiner uses — not how we all drove in 1995.

✅

See it pay off

Practice the plan, and the next lesson's score shows whether it stuck. No more guessing whether your teen is road-test ready.

The Practice Planner comes with every review — the private link in your email opens it.
