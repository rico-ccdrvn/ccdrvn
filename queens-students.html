Common Questions

Student FAQ

Do I need my own car?

No. All lessons use the instructor's dual-brake vehicle. You never need to own, borrow, or insure a car to take lessons and get road-test ready.

I have a licence from another country. Can I use it in Ontario?

Depends on the country. Some licences exchange directly at DriveTest; others let you credit your driving experience toward a shorter graduated licensing path. Bring what you have and we'll help you figure out the fastest legitimate route.

How fast can I get my G2?

With a G1 and a completed BDE course, you're eligible for the G2 road test after 8 months instead of 12. Provable foreign driving experience can shorten that further.

What's the AI lesson review?

An in-vehicle system records your drive and scores it against Ontario road test standards. After the lesson you get a private email with your score, clips of the moments that mattered, and study slides matched to what to practice next. See a sample report.
